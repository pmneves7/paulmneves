Common conversions

Convert between common energy, wavelength, frequency, temperature, and neutron scattering units.

Disclaimer: These tools are experimental and may still have bugs/errors. If you spot one, please tell me!

General energy converter

Uses E = kBT = hf = hc / λ.

Neutron energy ↔ wavelength

Uses the non-relativistic neutron de Broglie relation E = 81.81 / λ², with E in meV and λ in Å, v = h / (mλ), and k = 2π / λ.

Neutron momentum transfer

Converts between real-space distance and momentum transfer using Q = 2π / d. If the neutron wavelength above is set, it also converts to scattering angle using Q = 4π sin(θ) / λ.

Edit any field within a converter; matching fields update automatically. The general converter accepts SI prefixes such as meV, km, nm, kHz, MHz, and GHz. Frequency outputs use prefixes up to THz through 10¹⁵ Hz, then switch to scientific notation.
