Scattering calculator

Generate allowed Bragg peaks from a unit cell, space group, and wavelength. Optional scattering-plane HKLs add in-plane and out-of-plane angles.

Crystal and beam

Scattering plane

Enter two non-collinear HKLs in the scattering plane to calculate a signed out-of-plane angle. The in-plane angle is measured from the first HKL toward the second; HKL 1 is treated as the incident-beam direction for the rotation column.

Space-group filtering includes lattice centering extinctions and common principal-axis screw absences inferred from the Hermann-Mauguin symbol. When a scattering plane is provided, ω is the closest signed rotation about the out-of-plane axis for either symmetric detector side.
