Error propagation

Enter a formula to symbolically propagate independent uncertainties. Lowercase symbols carry uncertainty; uppercase symbols are treated as exact constants.

Supported syntax includes +, -, *, /, ^, parentheses, and single-argument functions such as sin, cos, tan, exp, log, ln, and sqrt. For example, y=A*x^2/z treats A as exact and propagates uncertainties in x and z.
